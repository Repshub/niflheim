Niflheim

Introduction

The small, sleepy village of Niflheim is located in a pine forest in the North. It's name means "dark world" and it's the sisterworld of Muspellsheim, which is located in the South. Niflheim is the mother of all creatures, as the icy water of Hvergelmir was responsible for the creation of Ymir, the first Giant. Yggdrasil, the Tree of Worlds, has one of his roots firmly settled into the ice-covered and foresty domaine of Niflheim.


Villagers

Niflheim is currently inhabited by nine villagers.
The favorite villager of Mayor Honey is living in a small hut just south of the Mayor's and is called by the name of Chevre. The goat chevre is a humble friend, always watering the Mayor's flowers and keeping track of the public gardens. One day Chevre's efforts will be rewarded, when the Mayor of Niflheim will pass on their duties onto her.
Most villagers of Niflheim are not permanent residents and will be adoptable when they want to move on from my settlement!
